About Us

It's robot fighting time! We are a rapidly growing group of roboticisists of all ages and skill levels coming together to bring robot fighting to North Texas in a big way. Our arena, the largest in Texas, will allow robot builders to test and compete in community organized events and competitions safely. Our goals are to foster innovation, spur technology sector growth, and grow the maker community in North Texas while having some fun along the way. We have partnered with the Dallas Makerspace, local businesses, and educators to do stuff. Connect with us via social media, view our facebook page , or contact us !
