About Us

It's robot fighting time! Dallas Area Robot Combat is a rapidly growing and diverse group of roboticists of all ages and skill levels who have come together to bring robot fighting to DFW. The D.A.R.C. arena will be the largest in Texas, allowing bot builders to test and compete in safe, local, community-organized competitions. Our goals are to foster innovation, generate interest in STEM education, and grow the maker community in North Texas, all while having some destructive fun along the way. We have partnered with the Dallas Makerspace, local businesses, and educators to help achieve our goals. Connect with us via social media, view our facebook page , or contact us !
